About me...

My journey with graphic design originated from my love for other artforms. From dance to music, I always have been driven to create. It is through those experiences that I found graphic design. My main inspiration has always been to be able to accomplish whatever artistic vision I have. Through this desire, I found graphic design and developed a desire to learn and grow as much as possible in this field. I am currently enrolled at Seminole State and am completing my A.S. in Digital Media with a Graphic Design specialization. After completing my degree, I aspire to continue expanding my experience and skills through freelance work.
