Safety Protocols

We work to make sure all of our staff is properly trained to provide your pooch with the most enriching and safe experience at The Dog House Doggy Daycare. We have built a program that not only provides socialization but also structured training time. During this time, dogs will learn and practice a variety of skills (sit, down, stay, shake, etc).

Staff Training

  • Staff is pet CPR certified
  • Staff has completed dog behavior training courses
  • Continued staff training and education

Pup Screening

  • Pre-enrollment Meet & Greet with Staff
  • Current Vaccination and Spay/Neuter Verification
  • Temperment and Skills Assessment

Ongoing Monitoring

  • Live Webcams all dog areas of our facility
  • Daily Reports verbal and written communication
  • Separation by size/temperment
  • Rest time in quite locations as needed
